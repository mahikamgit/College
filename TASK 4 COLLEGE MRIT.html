Welcome to Malla Reddy Institute of Technology (EAMCET/PGECET Code: MLTM)

MRIT - Creating better tomorrow...

Malla Reddy Institute of Technology(MLTM) is a constituent College of Malla Reddy Group of Institutions( MRGI).The Group founded in the year 2002 by Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy, an eminent educationalist, is the largest cluster of technical institutions in the state of Telangana (TS), with student strength of 28,000 and staff strength of 2500.

Malla Reddy Institute of Technology is established in 2008, Permanently Affiliated to JNTUH, Approved by AICTE and has a strong vision of offering world class training to the promising engineers.

The college offers B.Tech Programmes in ECE, CSE, & CE, M.Tech Programmes in Embedded Systems & VLSI Design, Computer Science Engineering, Structural Engineering and Transportational Engineering. More than 80% of the faculties are ratified by JNTU HYDERABAD. MRIT emphasizes on Application Oriented Teaching & Problem Based Solving. Since the establishment of the college it is known for academic excellence. In collaboration with Globerena Technologies Pvt. Ltd & Time Institute, the College offers Pre Placement Training Programs, e-learning & Training, e Assessment, Career Connect and Industry Connect Programs. Most of the MRIT students were placed in reputed MNCs like IBM, L&T IES, NTT Data, I Gate, HCL, Amazon & Genpact.
